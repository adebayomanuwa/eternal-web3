The Three Pillars

Three ways the layer remembers.

Footprints is built on three structural ideas. Each answers a different question about how culture travels through the £T€R₦AL ecosystem.

01 / Curated

Cultural Trails

Multi-stop journeys through artists, exhibitions, drops, and movements. A trail is an ordered sequence a visitor walks through — like a guided museum route, only the museum is the metaverse.

02 / Verifiable

Provenance Paths

On-chain ownership and transfer histories rendered as a clean ledger. Every Footprints item carries a verifiable path — who made it, who held it, who holds it now, and which trail it belongs to.

03 / Distributed

Creator Movement

A living atlas of where the work is being made. Static dots on a quiet map — each one a creator contributing to the ecosystem, mapped to a region rather than a precise coordinate.
